Chacon Beese Churger

Description

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ground Meat
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Condiments of Choice

Steps

  1. Raid your neighbors garden for required veggies
  2. Go to the store
  3. Purchase 5 Candles
  4. Purchase / Obtain Blood of Goat
  5. Perform Ritual of Goab, Lord of Gob, Demon of Oab
  6. Befriend Goab
  7. Invite Goab for a game night
  8. Betray Goab
  9. Butcher Goab
  10. Devour Heart of Goab
  11. Grind up meat of Goab and slice bacon of desired thickness
  12. Slice and prep veggies
  13. Grill bacon over direct medium heat, 350F - 450F for no more than 2 minutes a side
  14. Shape ground burger into paddies
  15. Grill over direct medium-high heat at 400F - 500F until it has an internal temp of ~155F, or as desired
  16. Throw everything together and presto - You have a yummy burgy :)
